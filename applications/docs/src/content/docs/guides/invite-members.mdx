Invites are how new people join your team. Each invite is a URL that, when opened, signs the user in with Discord and adds them to your roster.

## Generating an invite

<Steps>

1. Open your team → **Roster** → **Invite**.
2. Pick a **role** — player (default), captain, or admin.
3. Optionally scope to a **group**.
4. Optionally set an **expiry** (default: 7 days) and **max uses** (default: 1).
5. Copy the link and share it.

</Steps>

## What the new member sees

<Steps>

1. They click the invite URL.
2. Sideline prompts them to sign in with Discord.
3. They complete their profile (real name, optional jersey number).
4. They land on your team's dashboard with the role and groups from the invite.

</Steps>

## Single-use vs. reusable invites

- **Single-use** (default): the link becomes invalid after the first successful sign-up. Good for onboarding one specific person.
- **Reusable**: same link, multiple people. Good for seasonal recruitment drives. Set a high **max uses** or leave it unlimited; always pair with an **expiry** so it doesn't leak forever.

## Revoking an invite

<Aside type="tip" title="Revoke early, revoke often">
  If an invite leaks or you shared the wrong one, revoke it from **Roster → Invites → history**. Any future attempt to use the link will fail.
</Aside>
